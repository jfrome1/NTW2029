# Handbook of Learning Analytics : Introduction

There are 4 ways to understand Learning Analytics (LA)

1) LA as a concern or problem to be solved
2) LA as an opportunity
3) LA as a field of inquiry
4) The researchers and practitioners that make up the LA community


There are wide variety of interpretations when thinking about LA. Its literal deifinition is "analytics of learning".

"Learning" is like a universal experience, so it is open to interpretation by anyone, including a range of academic fields.
- I think this is basically saying that LA can be learned by anyone who's interested in this field.

The distinction between "analytics" and "analysis" is vague even among professionals
- It makes sense since during the 20th century, "analytics" was for pure mathematics, when they dealt with prime numbers.

So, it seems like the area of applied data analysis really is a recent thing
