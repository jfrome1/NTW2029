# General Summary of the Introuction of Learning Analytics (Handbook 1)

- The four main ideas of Learning Analytics (LA) : Concern, Opportunity, Field of Inquiry, and Community


<h3>Core Definitional Challenge</h3>

- The term "learning" is deeply debated, splitting between those who view it as an individual cognitive process and those who see it as a participatory cultural practice.

- Meanwhile, the term "analytics" historically referred to prime number research in pure mathematics rather than applied data, leaving the boundary between "analytics" and "analysis" heavily muddied.
