# Paper 5 : Using Google Analytics to Improve the Course Website of a Database Course

<h3>Introduction</h3>

- As online education continues to grow, instructors need to find new ways to enhance student learning online and to understand students' interactions with their electronic learning environment.

- Survey finding by Allen and Seaman shows that 90% of academic leaders beleive how it is very likely that students will be enrolled in at least one online course.

- However, as the usage of online tools increase, one concern arises. Instructors do not always have the liberty to observe their students in a face-to-face setting.

- Hence, there is a need for monitoring students' progress and capturing their interactions with online learnin content. The authors of this paper suggests that Learning Analytics(LA) is a good solution to address this problem.


<h3>Definitions & Objective</h3>

- "Analytics is about understanding, interpreting, and communicating data".
- In broad terms, data mining is "methodologies that extract useful and actionable information from large datasets".
- So, Learning Analytics refers to the collection and analysis of relevant data that students create when they interact with their learning environment.
