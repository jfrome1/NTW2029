# What I have learned and gained from Paper 1 : The Role of Learning Analytics in Evaluating Course Effectiveness

<h2>Application Categories of the Methodologies :</h2>
- There were total six categories : sentiment analysis, questionnaire analysis, engagement analysis, topic classification, predictive modeling, and performance analysis.
- This highlighted how AI algorithms helps educators analyze the data they have received and give them an idea of how they should refine their course.

<h2>Methodological Features :</h2>
- Student questionnaires was the most common data source. Learning Management Systems (LMS) and educational websites also serve as sources for tracking learner demographics, behaviors, and performance (which we already know). While descriptive analysis is the standard, methodologies such as machine learning algorithms can be used to do thematic analysis and sentiment extraction.

## I guess this paper was more about "how" analysis can be done using the general quantitative and qualitative data we have gotten from questionnaires. It does mention sometime about educational websites and the methodology to analyze them, but does not go too deep into it, which in our case is not that useful.
