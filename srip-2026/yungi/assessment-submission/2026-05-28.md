# Questions / Thoughts in General


### 1) How would we be able to determine if the student "actually" interacted with the source?

- As mentioned in the data-questino-mapping.md, a student may stay in the page for a long time, but not actually read nor interact with it. The pageview may suggest that the student stayed for a long time, but who knows? The student might have left the page opened while reading other sources from other websites, or the student may have daydreamed while opening the page for a long time.


### 2) What if the inactivity of a student is NOT because of the course website's quality? What if the student is just a person who cannot concentrate well? (watching youtube videos while reading, or being lazy and not reading / reading the page contents slowly)
