## Major Inquiries across Educational Research in the early 21st Century

1) What constitutes improvement?"
2) What should the relationship between data and instructor be?
3) How can the implementation of LA be done responsibly?
4) What role does the student play in the system?
5) And in what way will analytics aid in the development of Artificial Intelligence and vice versa?

<br>


<h3>A field of Inquiry</h3>

- Definition of "Inquiry" : Systematic Investigation, a study, or a line of intellectual questioning. So, basically when academics explore problems and try to find answers, they are engaging in inquiry.

- Important to ask : "What makes LA a field of inquiry in its own right?" I think this means what makes LA so attractive to researchers that it is reserach-able?
- What makes LA a "field" of educational research? What make LA be distinguished from other fields?

- The shared concepts of LA that holds the field together, is dependent on how we define the field itself. 

<h3>LA is a "human in the loop" argument</h3>

- although automation is powerful, education as a social enterprise requires human decision making.
- this has spawned inquiry into where and how humans and machines should interact in the processing and consumption of educational data through data collection, algorithms, dashboards, alerts, simulations, and/or policies
- this "human in the loop" in LA creates a level of internal conssitency.
