- Why the nutshells exist (context for the analysis)
  - The nutshell design solves a length problem: some assignments would run to roughly 15 printed pages if written out in full, which students would skip. Nutshells let a student scan an overview of the assignment and dig into each part's details as they work on it, without losing the overall context. A useful analysis would be any signal of whether that intended use is actually happening.
